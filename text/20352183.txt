The concentration of arsenic in the hair of the individuals living in Santana city was measured. 121 donors in urban and the periphery area were studied .
The periphery of the city shows the highest levels of arsenic ( 5.94 +/- 6.54 mg kg(-1) - mean ; 0.27-23.85 mg kg(-1)). 47.83% in the urban area showed arsenic concentrations of less than 1.00 mg kg(-1) , while in the periphery only 11.22% had results below this value .
The results showing the influence of residence location .
The Principal Component Analysis showed that the variable residence location were responsible for the correct formation of the periphery group .
