Daily intakes of essential minerals and metallic micro-pollutants are estimated from foods usually eaten in France .
These foods are grouped in nine categories .
For essential elements , intake estimates are comparable to the values recommended by the WHO .
The cadmium value is lower than the tolerable daily dose .
The estimated values are : cobalt 29 micrograms/day , chromium 98 microgram/day , copper 1.5 mg/day , manganese 2.5 mg/day , molybdenum 275 micrograms/day , zinc 14 microgram/day , aluminium 4.2 mg/day , boron 1.6 mg/day , cadmium 27 micrograms/day and nickel 231 microgram/day .
