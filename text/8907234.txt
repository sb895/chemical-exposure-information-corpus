Blood-fat concentrations of polychlorinated biphenyls ( PCB ) congeners : no. 105 , 118 , 156 ( mono-ortho-substituted ) and no. 77 , 126 , 169 ( non-ortho-substituted ) and PCB congeners no. 28 , 52 , 101 , 138 , 153 and 180 were measured .
The investigations were carried out in pooled samples from children and in individual samples from adults .
Additionally polychloro-p-dibenzodioxins and -furans ( PCDD/PCDF ) were investigated .
